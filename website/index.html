Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? expand_more

Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

How accurate is the AI transcription? expand_more

Our AI model boasts a 98.5% accuracy rate for clear audio in English. We also support over 30 languages with varying degrees of high accuracy. The Pro plan includes our most advanced "Whisper-Large" model for complex terminology.

Do you offer discounts for students or non-profits? expand_more

Yes! We offer a 50% discount for students with a valid .edu email address and for registered non-profit organizations. Contact our support team to get your discount code.

What happens if I exceed my limits? expand_more

We'll notify you when you reach 90% of your limits. If you exceed them, you won't be able to upload new videos until the next billing cycle or unless you upgrade your plan. We never delete your data for exceeding limits.
